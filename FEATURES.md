# SSO Tracker - Features

## 0. **Dashboard**
Your personalized home page showing a welcome message, today's date, and 4 quick statistics: Total Requests, Pending, In Progress, and Completed. See your active requests at a glance and quickly navigate to track or submit new requests.

## 1. **Filter by Status**
View requests filtered by their current status: All Requests, Pending, In Review, Processing, Completed, or Rejected. Click any status tab to instantly filter your request list.

## 2. **Filter by Date Range**
Use the "From" and "To" date inputs to filter requests by submission date. Only view requests submitted within your selected timeframe, and easily clear dates with one click.

## 3. **Ping Admin on Overdue**
When your request exceeds its processing time, a "Ping Admin" button appears. Click it to notify the admin and they'll prioritize your request. The button becomes disabled after pinged.

## 4. **Submit Request Notifications**
Receive instant toast notifications (3 seconds) after submitting a request, confirming successful submission with your document details.

## 5. **Overdue Alerts**
Get automatic notifications when your request exceeds its processing deadline. You'll see an "Overdue" badge on the request and can immediately take action.

## 6. **View Request Table**
See all your requests displayed in an organized table showing Document title, Submitted date, Status, Purpose, and Action options for quick reference.

## 7. **Multiple Document Types**
Request from 8 different document types including: Excuse Slip, Good Moral Certificate, Certificate of Good Standing, Enrollment Certificate, Indigency Certificate, Scholarship Endorsement Letter, Student Clearance, and Transfer Credentials.

## 8. **Notification History**
Access your Notifications page to view a complete history of all notifications: request submissions, overdue alerts, and admin pings with timestamps.

## 9. **Persistent Storage**
All your requests and notifications are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Your data survives page refreshes and browser sessions.

---

## Quick Start

1. **Submit a Request** - Go to "Submit Request" and follow the 3-step wizard
2. **Track Status** - Visit "Track Requests" to monitor all your requests
3. **Filter Results** - Use status tabs and date filters to find what you need
4. **Stay Updated** - Check "Notifications" for all activity updates
5. **Notify Admin** - Click "Ping Admin" on overdue requests
